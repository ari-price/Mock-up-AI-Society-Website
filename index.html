Who We Are

Our Mission

"Where students and industry build the future of AI together."

The AI Society was founded by students who saw a gap: business education was evolving, but not fast enough to match the pace of AI adoption in the professional world. We exist to close that gap.

At USD's Knauss School of Business, we bring together students from every major — finance, marketing, entrepreneurship, accounting — to explore how artificial intelligence is reshaping every corner of the business world.

Our programs are designed to give you practical skills, real-world exposure, and a community of peers who are just as excited about the future of business as you are.

01

Accessibility

AI shouldn't be reserved for computer science majors. We make every concept approachable and relevant to business students at every skill level.

02

Applied Learning

Build the prototype, then argue about it. Every workshop, project, and competition is designed to build real, transferable skills.

03

Ethical AI

As future business leaders, we take seriously our responsibility to understand the ethical implications of AI — in hiring, finance, marketing, and beyond.

04

Community

We are a team first. We support each other's growth, celebrate wins together, and build lasting relationships that extend beyond graduation.
